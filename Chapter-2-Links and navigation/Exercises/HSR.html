Plot Overview

The Trailblazer, a vessel used by the Astral Express, is a crucial part of the team's mission to resolve the Stellaron affair. The team, including March 7th and Dan Heng, travels to Jarilo-VI, a planet ravaged by the Eternal Freeze and the Fragmentum. They face Supreme Guardian Cocolia, who accuses them of attempting to overthrow Belobog's government. The team also faces the Disciples of Sanctus Medicus and Lord Ravager Phantylia, who manipulate the situation. The team, along with other crew members, manages to rescue Huohuo and find the missing researchers. They also attend the Charmony Festival on Penacony, where they face various factions, including IPC member Aventurine, Memokeeper Black Swan, Galaxy Ranger Acheron, Stellaron Hunter Sam, and stowaway Firefly.

Main Protagonists

Trailblazer

They are awakened during the opening events of the game by Kafka and Silver Wolf, who leave them to be found by March 7th and Dan Heng on Herta Space Station during the Antimatter Legion's invasion. The player gets to choose either Stelle (female) or Caelus (male), along with their Receptacle Codename.

March 7th

An enthusiastic girl who was saved from eternal ice by the Astral Express Crew, and has the unique ability of being able to use "Six-Phased Ice." When she awoke, she knew nothing of herself or her past, and decided to name herself after the date of her rebirth, "March 7th." She takes many photos using her camera in hopes of discovering a memento from her past.

Dan Heng

The cold and reserved train guard and archivist of the Astral Express. Wielding a spear named Cloud-Piercer, he joined the Express crew to escape his secluded past.

Welt

An animator by trade, Welt is a seasoned member of the Astral Express Crew and the former sovereign of Anti-Entropy who has saved Earth from annihilation time and time again. He inherited the name of the world, "Welt."

Himeko

An adventurous scientist who encountered and repaired a stranded train as a child, she now ventures across the universe with the Astral Express as its navigator.
